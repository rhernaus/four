The Four Number Sequence

The "Everything is Four" phenomenon is a fascinating mathematical curiosity based on word lengths. No matter what word you start with, counting its letters and repeating the process will always lead to the word "four" (which has 4 letters) creating a stable loop.

This pattern works in multiple languages where the word for "four" contains exactly 4 letters: English (four), Dutch (vier), German (vier), and others. Try different words in different languages to see how they all converge to four!
